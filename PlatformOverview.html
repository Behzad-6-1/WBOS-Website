A guided Operating System for the weighbridge

WBOS™ coordinates every step of the vehicle journey — bringing together hardware behaviour, driver interaction, compliance checks and data synchronisation into one cohesive operating system.

Configure compliance rules

Set axle group and GVM thresholds to support Chain of Responsibility obligations under the National Heavy Vehicle Regulator (NHVR). WBOS™ also captures the environmental and material details required for EPA waste contribution and levy reporting.

Guide the driver

Boom gates, traffic lights, terminals and cameras work together to guide the driver step-by-step through the weighbridge process. The entire workflow is designed so drivers can remain safely inside their vehicle.

Capture all operational requirements

WBOS™ records all critical information needed for operational, legal and environmental compliance, including:

  • entry and exit weights
  • axle group analysis and GVM checks
  • licence plate recognition
  • driver identity verification
  • material and customer details
  • photographic evidence of load and vehicle condition

Cloud-hosted or on premise deployment

Choose the deployment model that suits your organisation’s security and operational requirements:

  • Australian-hosted cloud — high availability, redundancy, encrypted storage, strong authentication
  • On-premise (offline) — full internal data control for organisations requiring localised hosting
  • Organisations may request to migrate between deployment models as operational needs change.

Define customers, products, required data inputs, business rules, safety steps and workflow logic to match how your site actually runs.

Secure, reliable communication

All site-to-cloud and device communication uses MQTT, a highly secure, encrypted and resilient communication protocol designed for industrial IoT environments. This ensures stable performance even in remote, bandwidth-limited or high-traffic conditions.
