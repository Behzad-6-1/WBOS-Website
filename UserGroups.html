The Operating System for weighbridge operations

WBOS by Logixis is a unified platform that coordinates drivers, weighbridge hardware, compliance data and business rules into one consistent operating environment. By controlling the full transaction flow, WBOS removes manual dependencies and enables organisations to run weighbridges safely, accurately and unmanned — even across multiple sites.

WBOS is designed with key industry stakeholders in mind

Weighbridge Operators & Facility Managers

From waste and resource recovery centres to quarries, council depots, manufacturing plants and distribution hubs, WBOS supports facilities that depend on reliable, compliant, high-volume vehicle movement. The system provides the automation, safety, auditability and data control needed for modern unmanned operation.

The Broader Weighbridge Ecosystem

WBOS is industry-agnostic and adaptable across all sectors using weighbridges, including: waste and recycling, environmental services, aggregates, agriculture, freight and logistics hubs, industrial processing, government and municipal facilities.

Drivers & Fleet Operators

Drivers complete transactions entirely from the cab using clear on-screen instructions delivered through the WBOS Driver App (iOS & Android).

This improves safety, reduces time on site, and streamlines the process during peak operation periods.

Organisations Managing High Volumes of Vehicle Movements

Businesses handling regular inbound and outbound transport flows gain a consistent, visible and controlled operating environment. WBOS improves accuracy, reduces administrative pressure and ensures every transaction follows the same guided sequence.
