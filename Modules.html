A modular architecture that scales with your operation

As an operating system, WBOS by Logixis provides a suite of integrated modules that grow with your facility's needs.

Preset builder

Define standardised transaction flows for repeat scenarios, improving consistency and reducing driver input errors.

Sales orders

Support customer orders with configured volumes, pricing, discounts and fulfilment tracking directly within the weighbridge workflow.

  • Learn More

Driver identity and access

Centralised driver registration, multi-site access permissions, organisation linkage and complete audit history.

Driver app (iOS & Android)

Secure mobile authentication, job allocation, notifications, transaction engagement, identity management and organisation requests.

  • Learn More

Core weighbridge control layer

The central coordination engine connecting:

  • weighbridge indicators
  • boom gates
  • traffic lights
  • driver terminals
  • cameras (ANPR, load imagery)
  • safety sequences and workflow logic

WBOS integrates with your existing weighbridge and hardware infrastructure, with no vendor lock-in.

Cloud transactions and reporting

A central system for searching, validating and exporting operational, financial and compliance data. Includes structured support for:

  • EPA waste contribution reporting
  • facility levy liability calculations
  • material category summaries
  • historical audit trails
  • financial and ERP export formats

Integration APIs

The WBOS API framework connects your operating system to:

  • ERP and financial systems
  • waste reporting systems
  • logistics and scheduling platforms
  • council databases
  • environmental compliance systems

WBOS strengthens your existing ecosystem rather than replacing it.
